# v0.3.0c — Synapse Usability Calibration ONLINE

Date: 2026-06-10  
Status: ONLINE  
Scope: Synapse Map interaction polish

## Summary

v0.3.0c improves the usability of the Synapse Map by correcting two interaction problems discovered during live testing:

1. Zoom was artificially limited to a narrow range.
2. Some visible stars felt decorative because their click-selection behavior was not forgiving enough.

## Confirmed Fixes

### Zoom Range Calibration

Previous effective safety clamp:

- Minimum zoom: 58%
- Maximum zoom: 240%

New effective safety clamp:

- Minimum zoom: 20%
- Maximum zoom: 500%

This allows broader navigation while preserving protection against map collapse.

### Star Selection Hitbox Calibration

The click-selection logic was upgraded from a flat hit radius to a dynamic hit-radius strategy.

New behavior:

- Tiny stars are easier to select.
- Guide stars are easier to select.
- High-weight stars are easier to select.
- Zoomed-out stars receive extra selection forgiveness.
- In overlapping hitboxes, the nearest eligible star wins.
- Blank space still does nothing.
- No zoom collapse or reset returns.

## Interaction Contract Preserved

Click = select  
Drag = navigate  
Release = release  
Zoom = deliberate  
Blank space = calm  
Words = beneath the map

## Result

The Synapse Map now feels more like a real navigable instrument and less like a decorative visualization.
